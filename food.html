Classes of food

Food is a substance consumed by an organism for nutritional support

Carbohydrate

Carbohydrate foods are all those with sugar and starch

  • Rice
  • yam
  • broccoli

Protain

Protein is a large class of complex organic chemical compounds that are essential for life

  • beans
  • banana
  • egg

zinc

A mineral is a natural solid with a definite composition and structure

  • beef
  • oysters
  • pumpkin seeds

vegetable

Vegetables are parts of plants that are consumed by humans or other animals as food

  • topato
  • squash
  • cucumber

fat & oil

Fat is needed to build cell membranes, the vital exterior of each cell, and the sheaths surrounding nerves. It is essential for blood clotting, muscle movement, and inflammation.

  • butter
  • choclate
  • cheese

Vitamins

vitamins are natural and essential nutrients, required in small quantities and play a major role in growth and development, repair and healing

  • walnuts
  • almonds
  • kale
